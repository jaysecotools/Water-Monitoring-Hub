About These Tools

These water quality monitoring tools have been designed specifically for Australian ecosystems, incorporating local environmental standards and guidelines. They provide:

  • Region-specific parameter thresholds for accurate assessment
  • Climate-zone adjusted recommendations
  • Specialized criteria for different aquatic environments
  • Easy-to-use interfaces for professionals and citizen scientists alike
  • Export capabilities for reporting and record-keeping

The tools are suitable for use by environmental consultants, government agencies, researchers, educators, and community water monitoring groups.
